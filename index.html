    • Cantonese electronic music producer Seablu(aka Scott Loong) is an everyman who loves Japanese culture and now lives in Guangdong, combining his unconventional sound, anime and video game influences with EDM. He made his breakthrough at the age of 20 with his debut single in 2022, adding Bubbles to his music with various sounds, including water droplets and sci-fi elements. In his choice of theme, Seablu's music adds more innovations to the original basic knowledge.
